The Process

From Vision to Bottle
in Four Steps

01

Choose Your Spirit

Select from our full private label spirits portfolio — bourbon, rye whiskey, tequila, vodka, gin, rum, mezcal, brandy, and RTD canned cocktails. Every category, every flavor profile, every format.

02

Design Your Brand

Our creative team develops your custom spirit brand — label design, bottle shape, and packaging. Premium glass bottles for shelf positioning, or sleek aluminum cans for the RTD market.

03

We Handle Compliance

Federal and state TTB approvals, label compliance, and licensing are fully managed by The Silent Group. We navigate the regulatory landscape so you don't have to.

04

Ship & Scale

From initial minimum orders to large-scale bulk spirits production runs, we grow with your brand. Start lean, scale fast — direct to your distributor, retailer, or door.
